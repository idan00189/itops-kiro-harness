# Product context

ITOps investigates production problems affecting a mobile application and its backend services.

Fill company-specific product, user-journey, ownership, severity, and SLO details in this steering directory or the local `wiki/` folder. Do not store credentials or raw production data here.

Primary outcomes:

- establish what users experienced and when
- correlate runtime, deployment, and data evidence
- identify a defensible root-cause status
- produce a detailed Hebrew report
- recommend next actions without executing changes
